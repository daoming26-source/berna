Introducing the Proactive "Drop Shield" Algorithm

This is the core differentiator. Our proprietary algorithm consumes real-time data to identify disengaged candidates *before* they withdraw, allowing for immediate, targeted intervention.

  • Real-Time Risk Score: Calculates drop-off likelihood (Low, Medium, High) based on time-to-completion, chat inactivity, and content engagement.
  • Sentiment Analysis & Drop Zones: Analyzes candidate communication mood and promptness in chat, triggering intensified engagement protocols when a candidate enters a high-risk **"Drop Zone"** stage.
  • Automated Engagement Prompts: Drafts suggested, personalized chat messages directly in the ATS interface for the recruiter to approve and send via the mobile app.
